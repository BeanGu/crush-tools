# Usage #

`range <options> <range_string>`



<range\_string>

 may be a mixture of comma-separated numbers and ranges (no spaces).

`example: range 1,2,6-10`

# Options #
|**Short Opt**|**Long Opt**|**Argument**|**Description**|
|:------------|:-----------|:-----------|:--------------|
|-h|--help|  |print this message and exit|
